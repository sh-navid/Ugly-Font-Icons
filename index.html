Home Page

As a start

This is the first website I designed after a long time. I tried to keep it simple and clean. I avoided any extra complexity and animations.


More details

This website is made statically with free and open resources. Glider.js library is used for sliders, Sansation is used for fonts, and JQuery is used to change objects. Images are cropped but their main source is Unsplash.
